FAQs

How are donors verified?

Profiles can include ID status, health checklist completion, last donation date, and hospital-confirmed donation history.

Can rural users receive alerts?

Yes. BloodConnect includes SMS-based alerts and voice-assisted access for low-connectivity areas.

Can hospitals manage stock?

Hospitals can update blood units, manage requests, create drives, and review analytics.
